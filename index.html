Welcome

Hi! my name is Dawid Joks.

I am a student at Confederation College, currently enrolled in the Interactive Media Development program. I'm taking a variety of classes including Mobile App Development, Interface Design, 3D Design, Motion Graphics, Web Design Development, and Digital Photo Imaging. The class that I am most passionate about has to be Audio/Video Storytelling, because I love to shoot and edit videos. I work with various clients creating video ads, as well as developing Web Pages.

Why choose me?

I am very easy to work with. I am a very organized person, and I'm happy to gain more knowledge in fields I haven't worked in yet.

I was born in Poland and moved to Canada at a young age. Polish people are known for being very hardworking, and I am no exception.

What I do?

I'm in my second year of a 3-year program at the Confederation College. While gaining experience and knowledge inside the classroom and through client projects, I try to get involved as many jobs pertaining my field outisde of school.

I currently hold the position as the Digital Media Production Project Coordinator at my school. What my job consists of is designing a new WordPress website for the DMP program, as well as creating video content to promote the program.

I am also invloved in creating promotional video advertisements for an upcoming sports matchmaking app company called WeSport. My tasks are shooting and editing commercials, as well as creating content for social media.

My Specialties

I can easily come up with creative and dynamic scripts for any commercial or video advertisement, shoot it, and put it all together. I have several years of experience with video editing through my YouTube channel, as well as school and real client projects.

I also have many years of experience with coding and web design. I have been studying it all the way from high school, through college and university, and using my knowledge to help real clients.
